We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to third parties, except when required by law or in compliance with legal procedures.

    Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize returning visitors. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

    Our website may contain links to third-party websites for additional resources or convenience. These websites operate independently from Kondhwa Namaz Timings, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

    We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We implement appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard the information we collect. While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

    9. Data Retention

    We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Once the information is no longer needed, we will securely dispose of it.

    As a user, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data stored on our website. You may also opt out of receiving communications from us by contacting us directly. Please note that some information may be retained for legal or security purposes.
